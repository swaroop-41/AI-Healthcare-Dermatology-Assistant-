# Medical Disclaimer

## Important Notice

This AI-powered Dermatology Assistant is a **decision support tool** designed to assist healthcare professionals in the preliminary assessment of skin conditions. It is **NOT** intended to replace professional medical judgment or clinical examination.

## Limitations

### This System:
- ❌ Is NOT FDA-approved for clinical diagnosis
- ❌ Should NOT be used as the sole basis for medical decisions
- ❌ Does NOT replace in-person dermatological examination
- ❌ May produce false positives or false negatives
- ❌ Has not been validated for all skin types and conditions

### This System IS:
- ✅ A preliminary screening tool
- ✅ Designed for research and educational purposes
- ✅ Intended to assist qualified healthcare professionals
- ✅ One component of a comprehensive diagnostic process

## Usage Guidelines

### For Healthcare Professionals:
1. Use AI analysis as a **supplementary tool**, not primary diagnostic method
2. Always perform thorough clinical examination
3. Consider patient history, symptoms, and risk factors
4. Verify AI predictions with your clinical judgment
5. Document that AI assistance was used in medical records
6. Follow established clinical guidelines and protocols

### For Patients:
1. **DO NOT** self-diagnose based on AI results
2. **ALWAYS** consult a licensed dermatologist for:
   - Suspicious lesions
   - Changing moles
   - New or unusual skin conditions
   - Any skin concerns
3. Use this tool only as directed by your healthcare provider
4. Understand that AI results are preliminary and require professional confirmation

## Accuracy and Performance

- Model accuracy on ISIC dataset: ~86%
- Actual performance may vary based on:
  - Image quality
  - Lighting conditions
  - Lesion type
  - Skin tone
  - Image angle and focus

## Privacy and Data Security

- Patient data is handled according to HIPAA guidelines
- Images and diagnoses are stored securely
- Access is restricted to authorized personnel
- Regular security audits are performed

## Emergency Situations

**Seek immediate medical attention if you experience:**
- Rapidly changing or growing lesions
- Bleeding or painful skin conditions
- Signs of infection
- Severe skin reactions
- Any concerning symptoms
